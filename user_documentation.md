# User Documentation: Douglas Real Estate Systems

Welcome to the future of real estate operations. This system isn't just a place to store data—it's a platform that executes work alongside you, powered by your Viktor AI employees.

---

## 1. For Agents (Active Amy)
**Goal:** Automate the grunt work so you can focus on closing.

### Getting Started with Leads
- **Auto-Sync:** Every new lead from your website, Zillow, or Facebook syncs directly to the Contacts tab.
- **Instant Follow-up:** As soon as a lead enters the system, your Viktor AI agent sends an introductory text or email based on your preferred templates.
- **Smart Reminders:** Viktor will notify you only when a lead responds or when it's time for a high-priority personal touch (like a phone call).

### Managing Your Pipeline
- Use the **Deals Kanban Board** to drag and drop your transactions through stages: Lead, Showing, Offer, Under Contract, and Closed.
- Viktor automatically updates the client's status in the CRM when a deal moves to a new stage.

---

## 2. For Investors (Scaling Steve)
**Goal:** Rapidly analyze deals and track your portfolio health.

### One-Click Analysis
- Found a potential deal? Enter the address in the **Property Analysis** tool.
- Our system pulls tax history, estimated rent, and market value.
- Instantly see your projected ROI, Cap Rate, and Monthly Cash Flow.

### Portfolio Dashboard
- Track your entire portfolio in one view.
- Monitor rental income vs. expenses.
- Get alerts when a lease is up for renewal or when market data suggests it might be a good time to sell or refinance.

---

## 3. For Brokerages (Broker Bob)
**Goal:** Oversee team performance and standardize high-quality service.

### Team Insights
- Access the **GCI Dashboard** to see real-time revenue stats for your entire firm.
- Filter by agent to see who is hitting their targets and who needs more support.

### Standardized Workflows
- Create a "Playbook" for your office. Ensure every agent's client receives the same high-quality follow-up by setting firm-wide Viktor AI sequences.
- Onboard new agents faster by giving them access to a pre-configured, high-performance system.
