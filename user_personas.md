# User Personas: Douglas Real Estate Systems

## 1. Active Amy (The Scaling Agent)
*   **Role:** Residential Real Estate Agent
*   **Background:** Amy has been in the business for 5 years. She closes 15-20 deals a year but is hitting a ceiling because she spends too much time on manual follow-ups and data entry.
*   **Goals:**
    *   Scale to 40+ deals per year without hiring a full-time assistant yet.
    *   Automate lead nurture so no one "falls through the cracks."
    *   Get a quick "at-a-glance" view of her commissions and pipeline.
*   **Pain Points:**
    *   Switching between a CRM, an e-signature tool, and her calendar.
    *   Forgetting to follow up with past clients for referrals.
    *   Manual entry of property details into listing presentations.
*   **How We Help:** Viktor handles the follow-up sequences and syncs data between her transaction management and CRM.

---

## 2. Scaling Steve (The Individual Investor)
*   **Role:** Buy-and-Hold / Fix-and-Flip Investor
*   **Background:** Steve has a portfolio of 10 rental properties. He’s looking for his next 5 deals this year. He works a day job and does real estate on the side.
*   **Goals:**
    *   Quickly analyze the ROI/Cap Rate of new listings.
    *   Manage multiple offers and contractor bids in one place.
    *   Track the performance of his existing portfolio.
*   **Pain Points:**
    *   Analyzing deals takes too long (manually using spreadsheets).
    *   Missing out on good deals because he’s slow to respond.
    *   Disorganized property management data.
*   **How We Help:** Automated deal analysis tools that pull in market data and a central dashboard for portfolio health.

---

## 3. Broker Bob (The Small-to-Mid Firm Owner)
*   **Role:** Principal Broker / Owner
*   **Background:** Bob runs a boutique brokerage with 15 agents. He needs to oversee the entire team's production and ensure compliance.
*   **Goals:**
    *   Monitor team-wide KPIs (leads, conversion, GCI).
    *   Standardize the transaction process across all agents.
    *   Provide value to his agents so they don't leave for a larger franchise.
*   **Pain Points:**
    *   No visibility into agent activity until a deal is already in contract.
    *   Agents using 15 different tools, making data aggregation impossible.
    *   Onboarding new agents is manual and slow.
*   **How We Help:** A "Single Source of Truth" for the firm with institutional-level reporting and standardized AI workflows.
